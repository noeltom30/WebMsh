WebMsh Product Vision Board

VISION

What is your purpose for creating the product?

To make high-quality CAD-to-mesh workflows accessible from any modern browser, so users can create, inspect, and export simulation-ready meshes without desktop installation complexity.

What positive change should it bring about?

Reduce setup friction for students, researchers, and engineers; shorten time from geometry idea to solver-ready mesh; improve confidence through interactive 3D validation and clear error feedback; democratize meshing workflows for learning and prototyping.

Target group

Which market or market segments does the product address?

Engineering education, academic research groups, small-to-mid engineering teams, and 3D printing/prototyping users.

Who are the target customers and users?

Engineering students, researchers, CFD/FEA engineers, instructors, lab admins, and technical leads.

Needs

What problem does the product solve?

Complex local setup, fragmented CAD-to-mesh workflows, steep onboarding, and poor error visibility.

Which benefit does it provide?

Zero-install browser workflow, unified geometry-mesh-export process, faster learning curve, and clear operational feedback.

Product

What product is it?

WebMsh is a web-based mesh generation platform powered by FastAPI + Gmsh and React + Three.js for geometry creation/import, meshing, 3D inspection, and multi-format export.

What makes it stand out?

Browser-first access, integrated visual workflow, and interoperability for simulation toolchains.

Is it feasible to develop this product?

Yes. MVP feasibility is already proven with working geometry creation, file upload, meshing, rendering, and geometry management flows.

Business goals

How is the product going to benefit the organization?

Expands adoption among users who avoid heavy desktop tooling, strengthens education/research reach, and creates a base for advanced capabilities.

What are the business goals?

Improve activation, increase retention, maintain high reliability, drive recurring usage, and ensure high import/export success rates.
